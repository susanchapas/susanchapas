| Mindless-Mirth-final.webp          | 2025 | iPad Procreate                                      | A memory of my younger sister and my godson, caught mid-laugh on the E train in 2018. Loud, silly, and entirely themselves, with no one watching and no reason to care. In the years since, puberty, social pressure, and a pandemic have changed them in ways I couldn't have predicted. Their laughter is quieter now; they second-guess things they once did without a thought. So this piece is less a record than a wish: to see them smile that carelessly again. Joy like that doesn't vanish. It waits for permission to return.
